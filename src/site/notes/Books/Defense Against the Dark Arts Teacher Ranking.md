# Defense Against the Dark Arts Teachers: Ranked
A tiered breakdown of each DADA professor based on **teaching ability** and **chaos factor**. 

---

## S‑Tier — Competent, Effective, Should’ve Stayed

### **Remus Lupin**
- **Teaching Ability:** Exceptional  
- **Chaos Factor:** Low  
- **Notes:**  
  - Best pedagogue Hogwarts ever had  
  - Balanced structure, empathy, and real‑world skills  
  - Monthly werewolf transformation still less chaotic than the average Hogwarts Tuesday  

---

## A‑Tier — Skilled but Problematic

### **“Mad‑Eye Moody” (Barty Crouch Jr.)**
- **Teaching Ability:** Shockingly excellent  
- **Chaos Factor:** Nuclear  
- **Notes:**  
  - Taught real defensive magic  
  - Also kidnapped the real Moody and groomed Harry for ritual murder  
  - Pedagogically gifted, ethically nonexistent  

---

## B‑Tier — Knows the Material, Should Not Be Near Children

### **Severus Snape**
- **Teaching Ability:** High  
- **Chaos Factor:** High  
- **Notes:**  
  - Brilliant wizard  
  - Would’ve excelled in this role  
  - Emotional intimidation as a teaching method  

---

## C‑Tier — Technically Qualified, Spiritually Chaotic

### **Quirinus Quirrell**
- **Teaching Ability:** Mediocre  
- **Chaos Factor:** Voldemort  
- **Notes:**  
  - Nervous and ineffective  
  - Also had the Dark Lord living on the back of his head  
  - Hard to run a classroom when your skull is a timeshare  

---

## D‑Tier — Should Not Have Been Hired

### **Gilderoy Lockhart**
- **Teaching Ability:** Zero  
- **Chaos Factor:** Catastrophic  
- **Notes:**  
  - Fraud who stole others’ achievements  
  - Released pixies he couldn’t control  
  - Removed Harry’s bones  
  - Attempted to memory‑wipe students with a stolen wand  

---

## F‑Tier — The Ministry’s Worst Idea

### **Dolores Umbridge**
- **Teaching Ability:** None  
- **Chaos Factor:** Fascist  
- **Notes:**  
  - Refused to teach defensive spells  
  - Tortured students  
  - Installed surveillance and authoritarian control  
  - Weaponized bureaucracy  

---

## Unrankable — The Position Itself

### **The DADA Curse**
- **Teaching Ability:** N/A  
- **Chaos Factor:** Infinite  
- **Notes:**  
  - Cursed by Voldemort  
  - No teacher lasts more than one year  
  - Hogwarts continues to treat this like a normal staffing issue  

---

## Summary
A cursed job, a revolving door of questionable hires, and a student body that somehow survives it all.
